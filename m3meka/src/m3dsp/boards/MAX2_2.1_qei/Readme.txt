Naming convention:
==================

PCB_MotorType_VersionOfThePCB_Robot

BDC: Brushed DC Motor
BLDC: Brushless DC Motor
0_3: MAX2 v0.3 Motor driver (w/ big inline connectors)
0_2: MAX2 v0.2 Motor driver (the original one)
